About Bolaram Railway Station

Bolaram Railway Station (station code: BMO) is located in Secunderabad, Hyderabad, Telangana. This station serves as an important suburban railway station on the South Central Railway network. Strategically positioned in the northern part of Hyderabad, Bolaram connects commuters to various parts of the city and surrounding areas. The station is well-known for serving the local community and military establishments in the vicinity.

How to Reach Bolaram Station

By Bus

Well-connected by TSRTC buses from various parts of Hyderabad. Multiple bus routes pass through Bolaram.

By Road

Located on Suchitra Circle - Alwal Road, easily accessible by private vehicles and auto-rickshaws.

By Train

Direct suburban trains from Secunderabad, Falaknuma, and other major stations in Hyderabad.
